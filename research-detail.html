Trial Setup Performance — Shelford Group

Source: Shelford Group data collection, April 2026. Metric: % of studies completing setup on target. Two snapshots shown: January 2026 and April 2026.

Methodology and data notes

Data is collected directly from each Shelford Group trust. The metric measures the proportion of studies that completed setup within the target timeframe. The numerator (studies completing setup on target) and denominator (total studies with known status) are reported by each trust. Percentage figures are computed as numerator ÷ denominator. The Shelford Group total is an aggregate (sum of all trust numerators ÷ sum of all trust denominators), not a simple average of percentages. The 'whole system' total covers all trusts reporting to this data collection, not just Shelford.

The denominators vary substantially between trusts and between time periods (reflecting different volumes of trial activity and different stages of reporting maturity), so percentage changes should be interpreted alongside the absolute numbers.

UK Clinical Research Delivery — National KPIs

Source: DHSC, UK Clinical Research Delivery Key Performance Indicators data tables, data to March 2026. Published April 2026.

About these indicators: DHSC publishes national KPIs tracking the UK's clinical research delivery system. These cover the pipeline from regulatory approval through to participant recruitment. The data covers all study types across the whole UK system (not just Shelford trusts). The 150-day setup target comprises three stages: MHRA/HRA combined review approval (target: 60 days), site opening after approval (target: 60 days), and first participant recruited after opening (target: 30 days).
